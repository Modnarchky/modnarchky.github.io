How it began

I’ve been drawing since a young age and have always enjoyed making things both aesthetic and functional. Over time, that interest grew into a passion for design — especially how visuals can communicate ideas clearly and create meaningful experiences. I studied Communication Design at the Glasgow School of Art and Digital Animation at Singapore Polytechnic, which helped shape both my creative foundation and technical understanding.
