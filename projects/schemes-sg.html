Key Takeaways

This project reinforced the importance of clear and concise storytelling in informative and training content. Considering the natual attention span of humans, it is critical to reduce the timing of the training video. The video is created for drug test collectors worldwide, and English may not be their first language. taught me how to work with limited resources and still produce a high-quality video. It also reinforced the importance of clear communication and concise storytelling in educational content, as well as the value of collaboration and adaptability in the production process.
